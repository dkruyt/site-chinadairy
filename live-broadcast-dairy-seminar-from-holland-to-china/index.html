Live broadcast dairy seminar from Holland to China

broadcast • Apr 14, 2017

Last Monday my company chinadairy.nl organized a live broadcast dairy seminar from Holland to China for Holstein Farmer magazine in China. In Holland we cooperate together with Comfort Cow Care, Grashoek Dairy farm and KI-Samen.

I was here the local organiser and host for the live broadcast to China. In China this was a first time broadcast live via satellite and internet for the Holstein farmer satellite channel. A select group in China gathered in Sino Farm, the same farm where also King Willem-Alexander visit before. The people there could ask live questions to Holland about cow care and dairy farm management.

Altogether there was a 2 hour broadcast and it was a great success. My company chinadairy.nl will organize for Holstein Farmer magazine in the near future more of these live broadcast dairy seminar in other locations and farms in holland.

Cynthia Zhu
