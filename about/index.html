About

China Dairy is a Dutch company who helps Dutch and Chinese Dairy companies to open each other markets. Helping with cultural and language barriers.

We can provide the following services for you:

  • Strategy advice and professional training  Dairy chain with partners
  • Provide specialists for congress or seminar
  • Products registration MOA (China)/NVWA(Nederland)
  • Help to improve your relationship with your Chinese partner or supplier
  • Find suitable partner or distributor in China or in Europe for your products
  • Management of the Chinese/European's Partner or distributor
  • Organising business meeting between Dutch and Chinese companies
